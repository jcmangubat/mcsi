Our school ethos: Guiding Children in Christ-like Character

At Mindawide Christian School Inc., we believe that manners reflect a person’s character. That’s why we take time to teach and model respect, kindness, and consideration for others, both in and outside the classroom.

Children are encouraged to say “please” and “thank you” in everyday situations, helping them develop a grateful and humble heart. We also emphasize Filipino expressions of respect like “po” and “opo”, which remind them to speak with courtesy, especially toward elders.

Rooted in Christ-like behavior, our approach promotes love, patience, honesty, and forgiveness. We teach children to treat others as they would like to be treated—just as Jesus taught us.

By forming both heart and habit, we prepare students not only to excel academically but also to become respectful, compassionate citizens of their community and faith.
